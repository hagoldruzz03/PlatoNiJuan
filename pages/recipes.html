Adobong Manok

Filipino Chicken Adobo Recipe

Chicken Adobo is an authentic Filipino dish and one of the most recognized Filipino foods. Not to be mistaken with Mexican adobo, this dish is uniquely prepared by stewing chicken in vinegar and soy sauce. The taste of juicy chicken in a succulent, umami adobo sauce makes chicken adobo a comfort food for all ages.

Prep20 mins
Cook45 mins
Total1 hr 5 mins
Servings6

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 lb) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2–3 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  2. Add onion and garlic to the skillet; cook until softened and brown, about 6 minutes.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar and season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf.
  4. Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender, 35–40 minutes.

Afritadang Manok

Filipino Chicken Afritada Recipe

Chicken Afritada is a type of Filipino chicken stew with tomato-based sauce. A simple dish perfect for lunch, best eaten with warm white rice. The basic version includes carrots and potato; this recipe is an improved version.

Prep15 mins
Cook45 mins
Total1 hr
Servings6

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic and onion in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce and 1 cup water. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add potatoes and carrots. Simmer for 20–25 minutes until tender.
  5. Add bell peppers, season with fish sauce, salt and pepper. Cook 5 more minutes.

Chicken Barbeque

Filipino Chicken Barbeque Recipe

Good food often leaves quite the impression after even just the first taste. Filipino chicken barbecue done right — with just about the perfect amount of savor and juiciness — possesses that same unforgettable quality.

Prep30 mins
Cook30 mins
Total1 hr
Servings6

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • ½ cup banana ketchup
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, banana ketchup, sugar, garlic, and pepper to make the marinade.
  2. Marinate chicken in the mixture for at least 1 hour (overnight preferred).
  3. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  4. Grill over medium-high heat, basting frequently with leftover marinade, until cooked through.

Chicken Caldereta

Filipino Chicken Caldereta Recipe

Chicken Caldereta is a Filipino chicken stew simmered to perfect tenderness in a spicy tomato sauce with potatoes, bell peppers, and green olives. Simple yet hearty — perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Prep20 mins
Cook50 mins
Total1 hr 10 mins
Servings6

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons liver spread
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • ½ cup green olives
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic and onion. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  2. Add tomato sauce and liver spread; stir well.
  3. Add water or broth and bring to a boil. Add potatoes and carrots.
  4. Simmer 25 minutes. Add bell peppers, olives, and chili flakes.
  5. Season to taste. Simmer 5 more minutes and serve.

Chicken Curry

Filipino Chicken Curry Recipe

The Philippines is considered the melting pot of Asia. This Filipino-style chicken curry uses coconut milk along with fish sauce and bell pepper — setting it apart from traditional versions.

Prep15 mins
Cook40 mins
Total55 mins
Servings6

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 stalk celery, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic and onion. Add curry powder and stir for 1 minute.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add potatoes and 1 cup water; simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, add celery and bell pepper.
  5. Season with fish sauce. Simmer until chicken is tender.
